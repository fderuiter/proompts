# Management Overview

## Workflows
- **[Learning and Development Workflow](learning_development.workflow.yaml)**: A workflow for creating a competency-based onboarding blueprint, a scenario-based microlearning series, and a training impact analytics plan.
- **[Project Management Workflow](project_management.workflow.yaml)**: A workflow to create a project charter, risk register, status report, and timeline.
- **[Study Director Workflow](study_director.workflow.yaml)**: A workflow for drafting a GLP-compliant study protocol, auditing raw data, and generating an executive summary.
- **[VP Statistics Workflow](vp_statistics.workflow.yaml)**: A workflow for creating an interim results executive brief, a statistical analysis plan, and a data quality risk heat map.
