## Workflows
- **[Agentic Coding: From Idea to Epics](agentic_coding.workflow.yaml)**: A workflow that takes a product concept, generates a product brief, and then creates project epics from that brief.
- **[Stochastic Model Chain: Architect -> Engineer -> Strategist](stochastic_model_chain.workflow.yaml)**: A three-stage workflow to model, simulate, and analyze conversation risks using Game Theory and Monte Carlo simulations.
- **[Technical Writer Workflow](technical_writer.workflow.yaml)**: A workflow for drafting a CSR results and safety section, an Investigator's Brochure summary of changes, and an SAE reporting SOP.
- **[Testing Workflow](testing.workflow.yaml)**: A workflow for end-to-end test discovery, design verification, human factors validation, and risk-based test case suite generation.
