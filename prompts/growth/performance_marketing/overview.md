# Performance Marketing Overview

## Prompts
- **[bayesian_media_mix_modeling_architect](bayesian_media_mix_modeling_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Formulates advanced Bayesian Media Mix Modeling (MMM) frameworks to estimate incremental ROAS, optimize multi-channel budget allocation, and model ad stock and diminishing returns.
- **[predictive_cac_payback_modeler](predictive_cac_payback_modeler.prompt.yaml)**: Synthesizes multi-channel acquisition data to model predictive CAC payback periods and optimize unit economics across the AARRR funnel.
