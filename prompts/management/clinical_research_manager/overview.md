# Clinical Research Manager Overview

## Prompts
- **[Accelerate Patient Recruitment & Retention](accelerate_patient_recruitment_retention.prompt.yaml)**: Develop a high-impact recruitment and retention strategy for a stalling clinical trial.
- **[Digest Regulatory Updates Affecting Protocol](digest_regulatory_updates.prompt.yaml)**: Analyze new regulatory guidance documents for impact on specific clinical protocols, differentiating between mandatory and recommended actions.
- **[Portfolio KPI Dashboard Brief](portfolio_kpi_dashboard_brief.prompt.yaml)**: Produce a one-page executive dashboard of enrollment, deviation, SDV, and budget KPIs for live studies.
