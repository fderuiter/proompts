# Executive Overview

## Prompts
- **[FDA-Aligned AI Governance Framework](ai_governance_framework.prompt.yaml)**: Draft an AI governance framework that satisfies regulators and sponsors.
- **[Crisis-Management Playbook Generator](crisis_management_playbook.prompt.yaml)**: Generates a high-stakes, legally precise crisis response playbook for critical data incidents, aligned with NIST SP 800-61 r2 and ISO 22301 standards.
- **[Digital Transformation Roadmap for Clinical Operations](digital_transformation_roadmap.prompt.yaml)**: Develop a high-level strategic roadmap for digital transformation in clinical operations, focusing on efficiency, patient centricity, and data integrity.
- **[Emerging-Science Horizon Scan](emerging_science_horizon_scan.prompt.yaml)**: Highlight emerging therapeutic areas or technologies likely to disrupt CRO services in the next three years.
- **[Executive-Ready Brief and Talking Points](executive_brief_synthesizer.prompt.yaml)**: Turn disparate reports into a concise executive brief and talking points.
- **[Executive Trial-Health Dashboard](executive_trial_health_dashboard.prompt.yaml)**: Summarize the health of active studies in a weekly dashboard.
- **[Hosting Cost Reduction ToT Plan](hosting_cost_reduction_tot.prompt.yaml)**: Develop a tree-of-thought plan to reduce hosting costs.
- **[Quarterly Innovation Radar for Decentralized and Hybrid Trials](innovation_radar.prompt.yaml)**: Identify and prioritize technologies for decentralized or hybrid trials.
- **[Investor and Board Narrative Builder](investor_board_narrative_builder.prompt.yaml)**: Craft a concise two-slide narrative for investors and board members.
- **[Strategic Market and Competitor Radar](market_competitor_radar.prompt.yaml)**: Provide an executive briefing on growth areas, competitor moves, and regulatory shifts.
- **[Regulatory and Competitive Intelligence Briefing](regulatory_competitive_intel_briefing.prompt.yaml)**: Provide a Monday-morning briefing on regulatory changes and competitor moves that may affect decentralized and hybrid trials.
- **[Strategic Consultant SWOT](strategic_consultant_swot.prompt.yaml)**: Generates a high-impact, board-ready SWOT analysis for Life Sciences organizations, delivered by a Senior Partner.
- **[Strategic Growth Roadmap](strategic_growth_roadmap.prompt.yaml)**: Rank therapeutic areas for expansion over the next three years.
- **[Strategic Market Foresight and Action Plan](strategic_market_foresight.prompt.yaml)**: Detect market inflections and craft a response plan.
- **[Strategic Portfolio Prioritizer](strategic_portfolio_prioritizer.prompt.yaml)**: Rank proposed clinical projects by scientific merit, ROI, risk, and strategic fit.
- **[Trial-Design Optimisation Memo](trial_design_optimisation_memo.prompt.yaml)**: Rewrite a study synopsis to accelerate startup while maintaining statistical power and budget.
