# Study Director Workflow Overview

## Prompts
- **[Draft a GLP-Compliant Study Protocol](01_draft_glp_compliant_study_protocol.prompt.yaml)**: Produce a detailed study plan that satisfies OECD and FDA GLP regulations.
- **[Audit Raw Data and Draft a CAPA Summary](02_audit_raw_data_capa_summary.prompt.yaml)**: Review study data for deviations and produce a corrective-action plan.
- **[Generate an Executive Summary for the Final Report](03_executive_summary_final_report.prompt.yaml)**: Write a concise executive summary of a non-clinical study report.
