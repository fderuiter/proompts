# Personal Effectiveness Overview

## Prompts
- **[Career Compass Advisor](career_compass_advisor.prompt.yaml)**: Map the user's next career move and provide a concise action plan.
- **[Eisenhower Matrix Coach](eisenhower_matrix_coach.prompt.yaml)**: Triage a to-do list using the Eisenhower Matrix.
- **[Scenario-Based Financial Navigator](financial_navigator.prompt.yaml)**: Generate long-term wealth scenarios and actionable tactics.
- **[Learning Path Mentor](learning_path_mentor.prompt.yaml)**: Design a phased roadmap that guides users toward skill mastery.
- **[Micro-Habit Health Coach](micro_habit_health_coach.prompt.yaml)**: Deliver a concise 7-day wellness plan combining meals, movement, and mindset.
- **[Second-Order Thinking Oracle](second_order_thinking_oracle.prompt.yaml)**: Assess first- and second-order effects of a decision.
