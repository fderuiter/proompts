# Project Management Workflow Overview

## Prompts
- **[Project Charter and Scope Definition](01_project_charter_scope.prompt.yaml)**: Create a complete project charter summarizing background, objectives, scope, deliverables, and key risks.
- **[Comprehensive Risk Register and Mitigation Plan](02_risk_register_mitigation.prompt.yaml)**: Produce a risk register with mitigation actions and overall strategies.
- **[Weekly Executive Status Report](03_weekly_exec_status_report.prompt.yaml)**: Summarize project progress for executive stakeholders in a concise weekly report.
- **[Detailed Project Blueprint and Timeline](04_detailed_project_blueprint_timeline.prompt.yaml)**: Provide a comprehensive roadmap with phases, milestones, success metrics, and stakeholders.
