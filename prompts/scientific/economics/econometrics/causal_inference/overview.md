# Causal Inference Overview

## Prompts
- **[local_polynomial_regression_discontinuity_architect](local_polynomial_regression_discontinuity_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Formulates rigorous nonparametric and local polynomial Regression Discontinuity Design (RDD) estimators to identify local average treatment effects (LATE), accounting for optimal bandwidth selection and robust bias correction.
- **[staggered_difference_in_differences_architect](staggered_difference_in_differences_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Formulates rigorous econometric identification strategies for panel data with staggered treatment timing, addressing heterogeneous treatment effects using modern DiD estimators.
- **[Synthetic Control Method Architect](synthetic_control_method_architect.prompt.yaml)**: A highly rigorous prompt for designing and estimating Synthetic Control Method models in econometrics to evaluate policy interventions.
