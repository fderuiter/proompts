# Clinical Outcome Assessment (COA) Prompts

This directory contains prompts for the development, validation, and implementation of Clinical Outcome Assessments (COAs), including Patient-Reported Outcomes (PROs), Clinician-Reported Outcomes (ClinROs), and Performance Outcome (PerfO) measures.

## Contents

*   **01_qualitative_interview_guide.prompt.yaml**: Draft qualitative patient interview guides for concept elicitation and cognitive debriefing.
*   **02_content_validity_clinician_input.prompt.yaml**: Establish content validity and inter-rater reliability through clinician input.
*   **03_psychometric_validation_methods.prompt.yaml**: Apply psychometric methods (Classical Test Theory and IRT) for validation.
*   **04_epro_migration_equivalence.prompt.yaml**: Assess measurement equivalence for ePRO migration.
*   **05_mcid_definition.prompt.yaml**: Define Minimal Clinically Important Differences (MCID).
*   **06_clinro_training_manual.prompt.yaml**: Draft instructions for ClinRO administration and training.
