# Coa Overview

## Prompts
- **[ClinRO User Manual Generator](clinro_training_manual.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a standardized user manual for ClinRO administration and training.
- **[Content Validity & Reliability Analysis](content_validity_clinician_input.prompt.yaml)**: Analyze clinician interview transcripts for content validity and plan inter-rater reliability.
- **[ePRO Migration Equivalence Checker](epro_migration_equivalence.prompt.yaml)**: Assess measurement equivalence for migrating paper-based PRO instruments to electronic modes.
- **[MCID Research and Summary](mcid_definition.prompt.yaml)**: Research and summarize Minimal Clinically Important Differences (MCIDs) for measurement tools.
- **[Psychometric Validation Methodology](psychometric_validation_methods.prompt.yaml)**: Apply Rasch and IRT models for COA validation and psychometric evidence generation.
- **[Qualitative Interview Guide Generator](qualitative_interview_guide.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a qualitative patient interview guide for concept elicitation and cognitive debriefing.
