# Psychometrics Overview

## Prompts
- **[item_response_theory_dif_analyzer](item_response_theory_dif_analyzer.prompt.yaml)**: A Lead Psychometrician agent designed to conduct rigorous Item Response Theory (IRT) parameter calibration and evaluate Differential Item Functioning (DIF) to ensure measurement invariance.
- **[network_psychometrics_architect](network_psychometrics_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Formulates mathematically rigorous network psychometrics analyses, estimating Gaussian Graphical Models (GGMs) to identify central symptom nodes and structural bridge pathways in psychopathology.
