# Control Theory Overview

## Prompts
- **[Adaptive Control Loop Tuning Architect](adaptive_control_loop_tuning_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Formulates mathematically rigorous adaptive control loop tuning algorithms for highly noisy, non-stationary dynamical systems under persistent disturbances.
- **[Robust H-infinity Control Architect](robust_h_infinity_control_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Formulates advanced H-infinity optimal control synthesis problems for multivariable dynamical systems subject to exogenous disturbances, unmodeled dynamics, and strict performance specifications.
- **[Stochastic Model Predictive Control (MPC) Architect](stochastic_mpc_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Formulates mathematically rigorous, robust, and stochastic Model Predictive Control (MPC) frameworks for complex dynamical systems subject to noise and uncertainty.
