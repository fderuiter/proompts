# Transcriptomics Overview

## Prompts
- **[differential_alternative_splicing_isoform_architect](differential_alternative_splicing_isoform_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Architects highly rigorous, statistically robust bioinformatic pipelines for quantifying and modeling differential alternative splicing (AS) events and transcript isoform usage from bulk or single-cell RNA-seq data.
- **[single_cell_rna_seq_trajectory_inference_architect](single_cell_rna_seq_trajectory_inference_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Acts as a Principal Computational Biologist to computationally model single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) cellular trajectories and infer pseudotime dynamics using advanced dimensionality reduction and stochastic differential equations.
- **[spatial_transcriptomics_cellular_communication_architect](spatial_transcriptomics_cellular_communication_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Acts as a Principal Computational Biologist to rigorously model spatially-resolved ligand-receptor interactions, predicting cell-cell communication networks across complex tissue microenvironments using spatial transcriptomics data and graph-based mathematical frameworks.
