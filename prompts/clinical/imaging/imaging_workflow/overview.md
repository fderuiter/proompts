# Imaging Workflow Overview

## Prompts
- **[Imaging Charter Draft](01_imaging_charter_draft.prompt.yaml)**: Create a study-specific imaging charter compliant with global regulations.
- **[Site Upload QC and Query Generator](02_site_upload_qc.prompt.yaml)**: Automate QC of imaging uploads and craft site queries.
- **[Central Reading Paradigm Design](03_central_reading_design.prompt.yaml)**: Recommend an efficient central reading model for an oncology trial.
- **[Regulatory Imaging Charter Generator](04_regulatory_imaging_charter_generator.prompt.yaml)**: Generate an imaging charter that satisfies FDA and ISO requirements.
- **[Image Acquisition QC Workflow Blueprint](05_image_acquisition_qc_workflow_blueprint.prompt.yaml)**: Design a site-facing SOP for image acquisition and quality control.
- **[Regulatory Imaging Data Package](06_regulatory_imaging_data_package.prompt.yaml)**: Assemble the imaging section of a PMA or 510(k) submission.
