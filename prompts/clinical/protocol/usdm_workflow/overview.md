# Usdm Workflow Overview

## Prompts
- **[Protocol to USDM Stage 1 - Metadata](01_usdm_stage1_metadata.prompt.yaml)**: Extract Study Level Metadata and Design from Protocol.
- **[Protocol to USDM Stage 2 - Rationale](02_usdm_stage2_rationale.prompt.yaml)**: Extract Objectives, Endpoints, and Eligibility Criteria.
- **[Protocol to USDM Stage 3 - Workflow](03_usdm_stage3_workflow.prompt.yaml)**: Extract Encounters and Activities from Schedule of Activities.
- **[Protocol to USDM Stage 4 - Concepts](04_usdm_stage4_concepts.prompt.yaml)**: Map Activities to Biomedical Concepts.
- **[Protocol to USDM Stage 5 - Assembly](05_usdm_stage5_assembly.prompt.yaml)**: Assemble the final USDM JSON from previous stages.
