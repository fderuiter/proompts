# Trial Execution Overview

## Prompts
- **[Adaptive Recruitment and Retention Strategy](adaptive_recruitment_retention_strategy.prompt.yaml)**: Design an optimized recruitment and retention plan for a multi-site pivotal study.
- **[AI-Powered Site and Recruitment Strategy](ai_powered_site_recruitment.prompt.yaml)**: Select optimal sites and anticipate dropout risks using simulated EHR insights.
- **[Compliance and Data Quality Monitoring Plan](compliance_data_quality_monitoring_plan.prompt.yaml)**: Design an AI-assisted monitoring plan to ensure compliance and data integrity.
- **[Diversity Action Plan Development](diversity_action_plan_document.prompt.yaml)**: Generate a Diversity Action Plan per FDA guidance.
- **[Informed Consent Process Optimization](informed_consent_process.prompt.yaml)**: Review and rewrite ICF for readability.
- **[Portfolio-Level Clinical Operations Roadmap](portfolio_clin_ops_roadmap.prompt.yaml)**: Provide a 12‑month roadmap for a portfolio of clinical trials.
- **[Protocol Optimization and Risk Simulation](protocol_optimization_risk_simulation.prompt.yaml)**: Evaluate a draft clinical protocol and simulate the effects of simplifying key elements.
- **[Patient Recruitment and Diversity Acceleration Plan](recruitment_diversity_acceleration.prompt.yaml)**: Boost enrollment and improve demographic diversity in a stalled Phase III study.
- **[Risk-Based Monitoring and Quality Plan](risk_based_monitoring_plan.prompt.yaml)**: Develop a risk-based monitoring plan for a Phase II oncology trial.
