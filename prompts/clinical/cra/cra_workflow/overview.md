# Cra Workflow Overview

## Prompts
- **[Monitoring-Visit Report Generator](01_monitoring_visit_report_generator.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a monitoring visit report summarizing on-site activities, findings, follow-ups, and attachments.
- **[Investigator Follow-up Email & Action-Item Tracker](02_investigator_follow_up_email_tracker.prompt.yaml)**: Compose a follow-up email to the PI summarizing visit findings and action items, plus a tracking table.
- **[Risk-Based Monitoring (RBM) Plan Builder](03_rbm_plan_builder.prompt.yaml)**: Develop a site-level risk-based monitoring plan with risk matrix, KRIs, and adaptive strategy.
