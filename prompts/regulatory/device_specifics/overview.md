# Medical Device Specific Prompts

This directory contains highly specific prompts tailored to particular medical device categories, assays, and diagnostic systems.

## Purpose
To provide detailed guidance on regulatory requirements for specialized device types, ensuring conformity with product-specific standards and guidance documents.

## Device Categories
- In Vitro Diagnostics (IVD)
- Next Generation Sequencing (NGS)
- Imaging Systems
- Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGM)
- Infectious Disease Tests (Zika, HCV)
