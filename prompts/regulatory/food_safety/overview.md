# Food Safety Overview

## Prompts
- **[Directed Food Laboratory Order Verification](directed_food_laboratory_order_verification.prompt.yaml)**: Review a Directed Food Laboratory Order to identify mandatory testing parameters.
- **[Establishment of Food Traceability Plan](establishment_of_food_traceability_plan.prompt.yaml)**: Create a structured traceability plan for a facility handling foods on the Food Traceability List.
- **[Food Safety Audit Reporting (Regulatory)](food_safety_audit_reporting_regulatory.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a regulatory audit report for an eligible entity after a food safety audit.
- **[Foreign Supplier Verification Program (FSVP) Audit](foreign_supplier_verification_program_fsvp_audit.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a summary report of an onsite audit for a foreign food supplier including conclusions and corrective actions.
