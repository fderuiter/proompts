# Food Safety Regulatory Prompts

This directory contains prompts specific to food safety regulations, including FSMA (Food Safety Modernization Act) and import verification.

## Purpose
To assist in the creation of food safety plans, traceability systems, and foreign supplier verification reports.

## Key Regulations
- Foreign Supplier Verification Program (FSVP)
- Food Traceability
- Regulatory Audit Reporting
- Laboratory Order Verification
