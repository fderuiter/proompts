# Compliance Overview

## Prompts
- **[Correction and Removal Reporting](correction_and_removal_reporting.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a written report to FDA for a device correction or removal.
- **[Cyber Device Security Plan](cyber_device_security_plan.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a postmarket cybersecurity plan and Software Bill of Materials (SBOM).
- **[IRB Protocol Review](irb_protocol_review.prompt.yaml)**: Evaluate a clinical investigation protocol to ensure it meets IRB approval criteria.
- **[Labeling Compliance Audit](labeling_compliance_audit.prompt.yaml)**: Audit device labeling for compliance with mandatory elements (Manufacturer, UDI, date formats).
- **[Medical Device Recall Strategy](medical_device_recall_strategy.prompt.yaml)**: Develop a mandatory recall strategy for a device posing health risks.
- **[Medical Device Reporting (MDR)](medical_device_reporting_mdr.prompt.yaml)**: Summarize an adverse event for mandatory electronic submission or develop an MDR SOP.
