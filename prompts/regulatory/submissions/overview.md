# Submissions Overview

## Prompts
- **[510(k) Substantial Equivalence Preparation](510_k_substantial_equivalence_preparation.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a substantial equivalence comparison and summary between a subject device and predicate(s) to demonstrate safety and effectiveness.
- **[Combination Product Jurisdiction](combination_product_jurisdiction.prompt.yaml)**: Prepare a Request for Designation (RFD) to identify primary FDA jurisdiction.
- **[De Novo Request Preparation](de_novo_request_preparation.prompt.yaml)**: Generate a summary of risks and mitigations for a De Novo classification request.
- **[Humanitarian Device Exemption (HDE)](humanitarian_device_exemption_hde.prompt.yaml)**: Explain why the health benefit of a HUD outweighs the risk of injury.
- **[IDE Application Preparation](ide_application_preparation.prompt.yaml)**: Draft an Investigational Device Exemption (IDE) application, including risk analysis and investigational plans for clinical studies.
- **[Medicare Coverage Request (IDE)](medicare_coverage_request_ide.prompt.yaml)**: Prepare a request packet for CMS reimbursement of an IDE study.
- **[Parallel Review Request](parallel_review_request.prompt.yaml)**: Draft an email requesting enrollment in the Parallel Review program.
- **[PMA Post-approval Reporting](pma_post_approval_reporting.prompt.yaml)**: Compile a summary of post-approval requirements, including clinical study data, manufacturing changes, and scientific literature.
- **[PMA Supplement (CBE)](pma_supplement_cbe.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a 'Special PMA Supplement - Changes Being Effected' for safety warnings.
- **[Premarket Approval (PMA) Preparation](premarket_approval_pma_preparation.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a detailed summary of a PMA application, including clinical investigation results and manufacturing history.
- **[RTA Checklist Preparation](rta_checklist_preparation.prompt.yaml)**: Annotate the RTA checklist with page numbers and sections where requirements are addressed in a 510(k).
- **[UDI GUDID Submission](udi_gudid_submission.prompt.yaml)**: Prepare a Device Identifier (DI) record for GUDID submission.
