# Regulatory Submissions Prompts

This directory contains prompts related to drafting and preparing regulatory submissions for medical devices and related products.

## Purpose
To assist in the creation of comprehensive, compliant submission packages for various regulatory pathways.

## Key Submissions
- 510(k) Premarket Notification
- Premarket Approval (PMA)
- Investigational Device Exemption (IDE)
- De Novo Classification Requests
- Humanitarian Device Exemptions (HDE)
