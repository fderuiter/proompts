# Regulatory Adherence Prompts

This directory contains prompts for ensuring adherence to specific regulatory guidances and frameworks, particularly from the FDA and ICH.

## Contents

*   **01_estimand_framework_builder.prompt.yaml**: Construct and implement estimands following the ICH E9 (R1) framework.
*   **02_dht_integration_checklist.prompt.yaml**: Review FDA guidance for digital health technology (DHT) integration and validation.
*   **03_multiple_endpoints_guidance_review.prompt.yaml**: Review and summarize FDA guidance on multiple endpoints and multiplicity strategies.
*   **04_imaging_endpoint_process_standards.prompt.yaml**: Review FDA guidance on imaging endpoints and create process checklists.
*   **05_rwe_framework_summary.prompt.yaml**: Review the FDA Real-World Evidence (RWE) Framework and fitness-for-use criteria.
*   **06_alcoa_c_data_integrity_checklist.prompt.yaml**: Ensure data integrity following ALCOA-C principles.
