# Administrative Overview

## Prompts
- **[21 CFR Part 14 Auditing](21_cfr_part_14_auditing.prompt.yaml)**: Audit advisory committee meeting minutes for compliance with record-keeping elements.
- **[Citizen Petition Preparation](citizen_petition_preparation.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a Citizen Petition requesting administrative action by the Commissioner.
- **[Civil Money Penalties Hearing Response](civil_money_penalties_hearing_response.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a formal 'Answer' to an FDA complaint seeking civil money penalties.
- **[Financial Disclosure Certification](financial_disclosure_certification.prompt.yaml)**: Identify required financial disclosure forms and draft attestations for clinical investigators.
- **[Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) Request](freedom_of_information_act_foia_request.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a request for records regarding a specific 510(k) clearance.
- **[Import Entry Data Element Compilation](import_entry_data_element_compilation.prompt.yaml)**: Compile required identifying information (510(k), UFI, NDC, NDA) for electronic import entry of drugs or devices.
- **[Medical Device Administrative Detention Appeal](medical_device_administrative_detention_appeal.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a written appeal for an administrative detention order issued on a food item or medical device.
- **[Patent Term Restoration Eligibility](patent_term_restoration_eligibility.prompt.yaml)**: Determine if a medical product's review period qualifies for patent term restoration.
- **[Privacy Act Auditing](privacy_act_auditing.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a notice for a new FDA Privacy Act Record System.
- **[Public Hearing Participation](public_hearing_participation.prompt.yaml)**: Complete a Notice of Participation for a formal evidentiary public hearing.
- **[Reclassification Petitioning](reclassification_petitioning.prompt.yaml)**: Prepare a statement of the basis for disagreement with a current device classification.
