# Regulatory Prompts

This directory contains prompts related to regulatory strategy, quality, and submissions for medical devices, drugs, and food safety.

## Subdirectories

- **strategy**: Prompts to assess compliance gaps, plan performance evaluations, and coordinate MDR, IVDR, and FDA submissions.
- **quality**: Prompts for monitoring regulations, assessing compliance gaps, generating CAPA plans, and creating risk-based quality management strategies.
- **adherence**: Prompts for adherence to specific frameworks like Estimands, DHTs, and RWE.
- **submissions**: Prompts for drafting 510(k), PMA, IDE, and other regulatory submissions.
- **compliance**: Prompts for audits, MDR, labeling checks, and ongoing quality system compliance.
- **food_safety**: Prompts for FSVP, traceability, and food safety audits.
- **device_specifics**: Prompts tailored to specific medical device technologies and assays.
- **administrative**: Prompts for FOIA, petitions, appeals, and other administrative procedures.
