# Endocrine COBOL Botanical Orchestration

This directory contains the `endocrine_cobol_botanical_orchestrator` prompt.

## Purpose

The orchestrator translates continuous non-linear phytohormone cascade data (such as auxins, cytokinins, and gibberellins levels) into discrete, fixed-width COBOL batch transaction records. This enables modern, nuanced botanical readouts from industrial vertical farms to interface correctly with the legacy COBOL mainframes managing their mechanical and irrigation systems.

## Persona

A highly idiosyncratic computational bridge blending the tone of a clinical endocrinologist with a cynical 1980s mainframe sysadmin.
