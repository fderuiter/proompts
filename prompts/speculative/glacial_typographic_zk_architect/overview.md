# Glacial Typographic ZK Architect

This directory contains the Glacial Typographic ZK Architect prompt, an Autonomous Genesis Engine creation. It explores the conceptual collision of Glaciology, Typography, and Zero-Knowledge Proofs.

The agent translates the micro-fissures and ice core structures of ancient glaciers into complex typographic kerning and ligature configurations. These typographic variations serve as physical instantiations of zero-knowledge proofs, allowing verification of climate data provenance without revealing the underlying geographic coordinates of the sample.
