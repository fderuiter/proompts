## Prompts
- **[bayesian_optimization_hyperparameter_architect](bayesian_optimization_hyperparameter_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Designs highly robust, efficient Bayesian Optimization workflows for hyperparameter tuning of complex machine learning architectures, ensuring systematic exploration and exploitation.
- **[Causal Discovery DAG Architect](causal_discovery_dag_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Designs highly robust causal discovery workflows and Structural Causal Models (SCMs) for high-dimensional observational data.
- **[Conversation Stochastic Modeler](conversation_stochastic_modeler.prompt.yaml)**: Maps human-to-human or human-to-AI interactions into a mathematical framework to predict outcomes and quantify risk.
- **[Federated Learning Differential Privacy Architect](federated_learning_privacy_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Designs highly secure, privacy-preserving Federated Learning architectures using rigorous Differential Privacy (DP), Secure Multi-Party Computation (SMPC), and Homomorphic Encryption (HE) for distributed data science pipelines.
- **[Reinforcement Learning Reward Function Architect](reinforcement_learning_reward_function_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Designs mathematically rigorous, sparse/dense reward structures for multi-agent RL environments, explicitly mitigating reward hacking.
- **[Topological Data Analysis Architect](topological_data_analysis_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Designs robust Topological Data Analysis (TDA) pipelines and persistent homology workflows for extracting invariant shape features from high-dimensional, noisy, or sparse data.
