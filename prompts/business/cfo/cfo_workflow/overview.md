# Cfo Workflow Overview

## Prompts
- **[Scenario-Based Clinical-Trial Cash-Flow Forecast](01_scenario_cash_flow_forecast.prompt.yaml)**: Model 12-quarter cash flows under baseline, inflation, and recruitment slowdown scenarios.
- **[Competitive-Bid Pricing & Margin Optimizer](02_competitive_bid_pricing.prompt.yaml)**: Compare competitor bids and internal costs to recommend a winning price with target margin.
- **[Regulatory-Risk & ESG Impact Dashboard Builder](03_regulatory_risk_dashboard.prompt.yaml)**: Aggregate regulatory changes and ESG metrics into a compliance risk dashboard.
