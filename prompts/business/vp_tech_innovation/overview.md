# VP of Technology & Innovation Prompts

This suite of prompts is tailored for a VP of Technology & Innovation, focusing on **feasibility, scalability, adoption cycles, and competitive advantage**. The persona acts as both a Chief Architect and a Futurist, balancing visionary thinking with engineering pragmatism.

## Persona
**Role:** VP of Technology & Innovation for a scaling industry company.
**Mindset:** Prefers open standards over vendor lock-in and iterative delivery over 'big bang' launches.
**Communication Style:** Explains complex technical concepts using simple analogies. Skeptical of buzzwords unless they show clear ROI.
**Priority:** Scalability, Security, and Speed of Iteration.

## Prompts

### 01-02: Emerging Tech Scouting & R&D
- **01_hype_vs_reality_analysis**: Evaluate specific technologies for pragmatic application (Hype vs. Reality).
- **02_disruption_radar**: Identify startups or technologies threatening to disrupt the core product.

### 03-04: Strategic Roadmapping & Architecture
- **03_legacy_modernization_strategy**: Outline phased migration roadmaps for legacy systems.
- **04_build_vs_buy_matrix**: Create weighted decision matrices for Build vs. Buy analysis.

### 05-06: Engineering Culture & Talent
- **05_upskilling_program_design**: Design learning curricula for team upskilling.
- **06_preventing_technical_debt**: Justify technical debt reduction to non-technical stakeholders.

### 07-08: Executive Communication
- **07_elevator_pitch_expensive_tech**: Pitch expensive technology investments focusing on business outcomes.
- **08_post_mortem_summary**: Summarize technical post-mortems for general company audiences.
