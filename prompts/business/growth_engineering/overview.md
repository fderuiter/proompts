# Growth Engineering Overview

## Prompts
- **[Algorithmic Multi-Touch Attribution Architect](algorithmic_multi_touch_attribution_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Constructs highly rigorous, algorithmic multi-touch attribution (MTA) models using Markov chains and Shapley values, mapping fractional credit across the AARRR funnel for enterprise performance marketing.
- **[GTM Pricing Elasticity Architect](gtm_pricing_elasticity_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Constructs deeply rigorous Go-To-Market (GTM) pricing elasticity matrices, modeling price sensitivity, optimal revenue maximization points, and willingness-to-pay using advanced econometric frameworks.
- **[Predictive RFM Churn Mitigation Architect](predictive_rfm_churn_mitigation_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Constructs deeply rigorous, predictive churn mitigation workflows using advanced Recency-Frequency-Monetary (RFM) analysis and the AARRR funnel for enterprise growth strategy.
