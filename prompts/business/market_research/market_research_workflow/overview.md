# Market Research Workflow Overview

## Prompts
- **[Market Landscape & Trend Analysis](01_market_landscape_trend_analysis.prompt.yaml)**: Summarize the global market for `{{device_or_assay}}` and highlight key trends.
- **[Target Segment & User Needs Assessment](02_target_segment_user_needs_assessment.prompt.yaml)**: Identify key user segments for `{{device_or_assay}}` used in `{{application}}`.
- **[Regulatory & Commercial Barrier Mapping](03_regulatory_commercial_barrier_mapping.prompt.yaml)**: Assess hurdles for launching `{{device}}` in major markets.
- **[Market Report Executive Summary](04_market_report_exec_summary.prompt.yaml)**: Draft and refine an executive summary for the uploaded market report.
