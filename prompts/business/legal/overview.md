# Legal Overview

## Prompts
- **[Automated E-Discovery Reviewer](automated_ediscovery_reviewer.prompt.yaml)**: Automates the first-pass review of legal documents for relevance, privilege, and key entity extraction during electronic discovery.
- **[Cross-Border Data Privacy Architect](cross_border_data_privacy_architect.prompt.yaml)**: A workflow dedicated to mapping data flows against overlapping jurisdictional frameworks (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA).
- **[Intellectual Property Claim Drafter](intellectual_property_claim_drafter.prompt.yaml)**: A prompt that enforces USPTO or EPO formatting constraints to translate engineering specifications into defensible patent claims.
- **[Mergers and Acquisitions Due Diligence Auditor](mergers_acquisitions_due_diligence_auditor.prompt.yaml)**: An agent designed to process data room artifacts, flag indemnity risks, and generate risk matrices for asset purchase agreements.
