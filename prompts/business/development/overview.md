# Development Overview

## Prompts
- **[90-Day Pipeline Health & Next-Best-Action Review](90_day_pipeline_health_review.prompt.yaml)**: Assess the health of the sales pipeline and recommend next-best actions.
- **[Competitor-Positioning Brief](competitor_positioning_brief.prompt.yaml)**: Provide a competitor comparison to highlight differentiators for an upcoming board review.
- **[Emerging-Market Opportunity Scan](emerging_market_opportunity_scan.prompt.yaml)**: Identify high-growth therapeutic areas or sponsor segments for CRO services.
- **[Market-Intelligence Radar](market_intelligence_radar.prompt.yaml)**: Prioritize high-potential biotech or pharma companies for partnership opportunities.
- **[Marketing Campaign for Clinical Services](marketing_campaign_in_silico.prompt.yaml)**: Generates a comprehensive marketing asset package including landing page copy, email sequence, and sell sheet for new clinical services.
- **[Rapid Proposal Builder](rapid_proposal_builder.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a concise capabilities and budget proposal for a prospective client.
- **[RFP Executive-Summary Generator](rfp_executive_summary_generator.prompt.yaml)**: Produce a persuasive executive summary for an RFP response.
- **[Strategic Business Case for New Service Line](strategic_business_case_in_silico.prompt.yaml)**: Drafts a comprehensive New Venture Strategic Plan for a new service line, focusing on market shift, service breakdown, SWOT, target client, and roadmap.
