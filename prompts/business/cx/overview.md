# Cx Overview

## Prompts
- **[CSM Portfolio Balancing](csm_portfolio_balancing.prompt.yaml)**: Propose a weighted scoring model to balance account loads among CSMs.
- **[Friction-Hunting Onboarding Audit](onboarding_audit.prompt.yaml)**: Critique onboarding steps to identify friction and propose low-touch alternatives.
- **[Red Account Turnaround Strategy](red_account_turnaround.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a re-engagement playbook for at-risk high-value accounts.
- **[Cross-Functional Advocacy Memo](revenue_risk_advocacy.prompt.yaml)**: Draft a memo to Product/Sales focusing on revenue risk to prioritize features.
- **[Voice of Customer Root Cause Analysis](root_cause_analysis.prompt.yaml)**: Analyze raw feedback to identify root causes and quick wins.
- **[Trend Spotting vs Anomalies](trend_spotting.prompt.yaml)**: Compare support ticket datasets to identify trends and anomalies.
- **[Value-Based QBR Generator](value_based_qbr.prompt.yaml)**: Create a concise, impact-focused Quarterly Business Review template.
