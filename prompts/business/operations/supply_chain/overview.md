# Supply Chain Overview

## Prompts
- **[global_supply_chain_geopolitical_duress_architect](global_supply_chain_geopolitical_duress_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Formulates robust, mathematically rigorous global supply chain rerouting and resilience frameworks under severe geopolitical shocks and trade duress.
- **[Global Supply Chain Resilience Architect](global_supply_chain_resilience_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Designs highly rigorous, quantitative supply chain network optimization models to mitigate geopolitical duress, port strikes, tariffs, and route closures using advanced operations research frameworks.
- **[Stochastic Inventory EOQ Optimization Architect](stochastic_inventory_eoq_optimization_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Architects rigorous, quantitative inventory optimization frameworks using stochastic Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) and Safety Stock modeling to minimize holding costs and eliminate stockouts under demand uncertainty.
- **[Supply Chain Network Topology Optimization Architect](supply_chain_network_topology_optimization_architect.prompt.yaml)**: Architects mathematically rigorous supply chain network topologies, optimizing facility location, capacity allocation, and distribution flows under multi-echelon constraints using mixed-integer linear programming (MILP) frameworks.
