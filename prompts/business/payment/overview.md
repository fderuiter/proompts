# Payment Overview

## Prompts
- **[Build an Audit-Ready Site-Payment Schedule](audit_ready_site_payment_schedule.prompt.yaml)**: Generate a transparent investigator payment schedule that withstands audit review.
- **[Medicare Coverage Analysis](coverage_analysis.prompt.yaml)**: Determine qualifying status and billing justification.
- **[Global Regulatory and Tax Matrix for Site Payments](global_regulatory_tax_matrix.prompt.yaml)**: Summarize key payment compliance requirements across major regions.
- **[Investigator-Site Payment Forecast](investigator_site_payment_forecast.prompt.yaml)**: Produce a month-by-month cash-flow forecast for site payments.
- **[Payment-Process Risk Assessment and Mitigation](payment_process_risk_assessment.prompt.yaml)**: Identify weak points in the site-payment workflow and propose mitigations.
- **[Payment Reconciliation and Discrepancy Report](payment_reconciliation_discrepancy_report.prompt.yaml)**: Identify and categorize payment discrepancies before study close-out.
- **[Sunshine Act and FMV Compliance Check](sunshine_act_fmv_compliance_check.prompt.yaml)**: Audit site-payment data for Sunshine Act reporting and FMV adherence.
